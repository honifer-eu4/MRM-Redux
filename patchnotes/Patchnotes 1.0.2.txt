MRM Redux Patch 1.0.2:

################### General ##################

Decisions:
    Decision added to purchase a second colonist, costing 1500 ducats and giving a permanent extra colonist. This decision is available after colonialism is embraced and the first colonization decision has been taken.
    "Potential Formable Information" now works for colonial nations.

Institutions:
    Colonialism spawn requirement changed from having Exploration Ideas to having Interest in Colonies.

############# Government Reforms #############

Colonial Government:
    Now has +1 Colonist.

################## Missions ##################

Denmark:
    Mission tree updated to allow access to Carolean Infantry after forming Scandinavia.
